<p><b>Bush</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> in northeastern <a href="/wiki/St._Tammany_Parish,_Louisiana" title="St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana">St. Tammany Parish</a>, <a href="/wiki/Louisiana" title="Louisiana">Louisiana</a>, United States, to the south of <a href="/wiki/Sun,_Louisiana" title="Sun, Louisiana">Sun</a>. Bush is on <a href="/wiki/Louisiana_Highway_21" title="Louisiana Highway 21">Louisiana Highway 21</a> between <a href="/wiki/Covington,_Louisiana" title="Covington, Louisiana">Covington</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bogalusa,_Louisiana" title="Bogalusa, Louisiana">Bogalusa</a> at 30,61° N, 89,90° W. It is part of the <a href="/wiki/New_Orleans,_Louisiana" class="mw-redirect" title="New Orleans, Louisiana">New Orleans</a>&#8211;<a href="/wiki/Metairie,_Louisiana" title="Metairie, Louisiana">Metairie</a>&#8211;<a href="/wiki/Kenner,_Louisiana" title="Kenner, Louisiana">Kenner</a> <a href="/wiki/New_Orleans_metropolitan_area" title="New Orleans metropolitan area">Metropolitan Statistical Area</a>. Highway 21 crosses the <a href="/wiki/Bogue_Chitto_River" title="Bogue Chitto River">Bogue Chitto River</a> approximately 1.45 miles (2.33&#160;km) north of town.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bush,_Louisiana&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/St._Tammany_Parish_Public_Schools" title="St. Tammany Parish Public Schools">St. Tammany Parish Public Schools</a> serves Bush residents.
</p><p>Fifth Ward Junior High School in unincorporated St. Tammany Parish serves K-8.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Covington_High_School_(Covington,_Louisiana)" title="Covington High School (Covington, Louisiana)">Covington High School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Covington,_Louisiana" title="Covington, Louisiana">Covington</a> serves Bush residents.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
